How it works

Simple. Secure. Human.

Attach

Place your Bag.ID tag on your luggage. Register the code once. You’re set.

Scan

If found, anyone can scan or enter the code. No app. No login. Just honesty.

Reunite

You receive a message and optional photo. Coordinate safely. Finder details auto-delete.

Privacy, by design

  • ✓ Finder details are automatically deleted after 7 days — always.
  • ✓ No tracking. No accounts. No ads. Just a secure message relay.
  • ✓ Your contact details are never shown to a finder unless you choose to share them.
