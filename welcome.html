Welcome

Thanks for scanning — this is Bag.ID

Bag.ID helps people keep what’s theirs and return what’s lost — without tracking, profiling, or selling anyone’s data.

Every Bag.ID tag has a unique QR and a simple orange dot. When it’s scanned, we provide a safe way for a finder to send a message to the owner. That’s it.

How it works

  • Scan the QR or enter the code on bag.id.
  • Send a message to the owner through our secure form (no account needed).
  • Coordinate return privately — owner details are never shown publicly.

Our privacy promise

We only keep what’s needed to connect a finder with an owner. No location tracking, no ads, no profiling.

Want to verify a bag without a QR? Look for the orange dot inside the bag — it shows a simple CCV number used for backup verification.
