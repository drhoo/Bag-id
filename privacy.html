Privacy Policy

Bag.ID is built on trust. We only collect the information needed to reunite a lost bag with its owner — nothing more, nothing less.

1. What we collect

When you activate a tag, we store your Bag.ID code and your contact email. If someone reports a found item, we temporarily store their message and optional contact details.

2. How we use it

Finder messages are securely relayed to the registered owner. The owner can choose whether to reply directly to the finder.

3. What we delete

Finder details and messages are automatically deleted after 7 days — always. Owner records remain only so long as you keep your tag active.

4. What we never do

  • We never track your location.
  • We never sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties.
  • We never display your personal details to anyone without consent.

5. Our principles

Bag.ID follows the Pebble System principles of simplicity, transparency, and respect for privacy. Every process is designed to be clear, human, and limited in scope.

6. Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@bag.id.
